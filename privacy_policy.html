动森岛屿建设（Island Designer）

概述

动森岛屿建设（Island Designer）是一款完全离线运行的岛屿地图编辑器应用。我们高度重视您的隐私，本政策将说明我们如何处理您的信息。

信息收集

我们不收集任何个人信息。

本应用不要求注册账号，不收集姓名、邮箱、电话号码或任何其他个人身份信息。应用不包含任何用户追踪、分析工具或广告 SDK。

数据存储

您在应用中创建的所有地图数据均仅保存在您的设备本地。我们无法访问、查看或获取您的任何数据。

网络访问

本应用不需要网络连接即可正常运行。应用不会向任何服务器发送数据。

第三方服务

本应用不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

儿童隐私

本应用不收集任何用户的个人信息，因此适合所有年龄段的用户使用。

🏝️ Privacy Policy

Island Designer

Overview

Island Designer is a fully offline island map editor application. We take your privacy seriously, and this policy explains how we handle your information.

Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information.

This app does not require account registration and does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information. The app contains no user tracking, analytics tools, or advertising SDKs.

Data Storage

All map data you create in the app is stored exclusively on your local device. We cannot access, view, or retrieve any of your data.

Network Access

This app does not require an internet connection to function. The app does not send any data to any server.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This app does not collect personal information from any user, making it suitable for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted on this page. We recommend checking this page periodically for the latest information.
